# NorthStar Immigration

**NorthStar Immigration** is a simple app that connects low-income and underrepresented immigrants to affordable legal help, community programs, and answers to their toughest questions related to immigration. Like the North Star in the night sky, the app will always be there to guide you, no matter where you are on your journey.

---

## App Overview

Using the **menu bar at the bottom**, users can:

- Search for trusted immigration lawyers  
- Explore local community resources  
- Find clear, reliable answers to the questions immigrants ask most  

### 🔍 Lawyer Search Page

- Filter results by **language, location, and cost** to find the best lawyer.  
- Each listing displays:  
  - Law firm name  
  - Location  
  - Languages spoken  
  - Pricing  
  - Areas of specialization  
  - Direct link to contact the lawyer  

When a user clicks the contact link, they enter their **name, contact information, and a short message**. Once submitted, the information is securely sent to a spreadsheet, allowing the team to forward the message to the lawyer — making the process simple and efficient.

### 🏠 Community Programs Page

- Filter results based on **services and location**.  
- Each listing provides:  
  - Organization name  
  - Location  
  - Services offered  
  - Website  
  - Contact information  

This helps users connect with groups that can ease their transition into life in America.

### ❓ Immigration FAQ Page

- Click on each question to reveal a dropdown with clear, concise answers.  
- Offers guidance on common topics immigrants often face.

### 🌐 Multilingual Support

- Users can select their preferred language from a **dropdown menu in the top-right corner**.  
- Currently available in **English, Spanish, Hindi, Chinese, and Ukrainian**.  
- Future plans include adding even more languages to expand our impact.

---

## Technical Aspects

**NorthStar Immigration** is built using **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**, with GitHub and VS Code for development.

- **Filters** on the Lawyer Search and Community Programs pages use JavaScript to dynamically update results.  
- **Contact form** uses Google Script to securely send data to the correct lawyer.  
- **FAQ section** uses JavaScript to toggle answers for an interactive experience.  
- **Language selection** reloads the page and updates the app files to the corresponding language version.  
- **Responsive CSS design** ensures the app adapts seamlessly to any device, from laptops to mobile phones.
